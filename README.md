Ride Booking & Cancellation Analytics (OLA and Uber),SQL Project

• Analyzed 1 lac plus ride bookings using SQL to evaluate booking performance, identifying a 62% ridesuccess rate and 38% revenue-impacting failures.

• Performed cancellation root-cause analysis, revealing driver cancellations about 18% as the primarycontributor, among which 35.5% of driver cancellation occur due to personal and car related issues.

• Assessed vehicle efficiency and turnaround times, showing sedans as the most reliable while e-Bikesand autos had the highest cancellation rates around 28%.

• Conducted time-based and location-based analysis to identify peak demand hours, high-riskcancellation windows (6–9 PM), and high-cancellation pickup/drop zones.

• Executed customer and revenue analytics, identifying 9,178 high-risk customers (cancellation rate>50%) and analyzing $56.5M total revenue.
